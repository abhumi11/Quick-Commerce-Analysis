- Project Title / Headline
Quick Commerce Analysis | End-to-End Sales, Customer & Delivery Analysis using SQL, Excel, Python & Power BI

- Short Description / Purpose
This project delivers an end-to-end Analytical solution for a Quick Commerce company using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI. It covers data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business KPI analysis, and interactive dashboard development to uncover insights into sales, customer behavior, product performance, delivery efficiency, and profitability. The project enables data-driven decision-making by transforming raw business data into actionable insights and strategic recommendations.

- Features & Dashboard Highlights
  
--Business Problem

Quick Commerce companies process thousands of orders every day, making it challenging to monitor sales performance, delivery operations, customer behavior, and product profitability in a unified manner. Without a centralized analytics solution, businesses struggle to identify high-performing products and cities, optimize delivery efficiency, evaluate the impact of discounts, and track key performance indicators. This project addresses these challenges by leveraging Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze transactional data, generate actionable business insights, and provide interactive dashboards that support data-driven decision-making, improve operational efficiency, and drive sustainable business growth.

--Goal of the Project

Increase overall revenue by identifying sales trends and growth opportunities.
Improve profitability by analyzing product performance, costs, and discount strategies.
Optimize delivery operations to reduce delivery time and improve customer satisfaction.
Understand customer purchasing behavior to enhance retention and increase repeat orders.
Identify top-performing and underperforming cities for better market expansion and resource allocation.
Track key business KPIs through interactive dashboards for faster and data-driven decision-making.
Evaluate the impact of pricing, discounts, and payment methods on business performance.
Provide actionable insights to support strategic planning and improve operational efficiency.

-- Project Walkthrough

 Step 1: Data Preparation (Excel)

Imported the Quick Commerce dataset, cleaned missing values, standardized data formats, created calculated fields, and prepared the dataset for further analysis.

 Step 2: Business Analysis (SQL)

Performed sales, customer, product, delivery, city, and overall business analysis using SQL queries to extract KPIs and generate business insights.

 Step 3: Exploratory Data Analysis & Machine Learning (Python)

Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends and patterns. Built machine learning models for customer segmentation, sales prediction, and order status prediction.

  Step 4: Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPI cards, charts, maps, and slicers to visualize sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, delivery efficiency, and city-wise performance.

  Step 5: Business Insights

Generated actionable insights and recommendations to improve profitability, optimize delivery operations, enhance customer experience, and support data-driven business decisions.
